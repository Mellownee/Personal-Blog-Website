The line horror and thriller is blurry and for another post. For this post we will keep discussion light with supernatural horror/thrillers. Some will have you invite your friend or kids to a last minute slumber party. Others, will make you laugh or cry but they all have one thing in common. A variable to an amazing horror film. The common sense factor. The key to a good horror film is a villan that we relate to, a protagonist that doesn't trip while running, a slow enough pace so that we can know your character enough to care, a character that does what we would or at least what we can understand.
